Chicken Street Tacos

About the recipe

This simple street taco recipe uses delicious preseasoned meat known as pollo para asar combined with the time-honored combination of onions and cilantro to make a flavorful, easy dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb. pollo para asar (Seasoned, boneless, skinless chicken.)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro
  • 8 corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat and oil the grate.
  2. Grill chicken, turning occasionally until temperature reaches at least 165 degrees F.
  3. Heat corn tortillas until soft. Top with chicken, onions and cilantro.
